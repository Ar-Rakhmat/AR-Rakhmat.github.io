About Me

I am an architect from the city of Saratov. In 2008 he graduated with honors from PGMSK with a degree in Architecture with the qualification of a technician. In 2014 he graduated with honors from SSTU. Gagarina Yu.A. in the specialty "Architecture" with the qualification "architect". I have been in the design business since 2011. The directions of BIM design and parametric architecture are a priority for me. I will consider job offers where knowledge of BIM design and node based programming is required.
